Leah Steinberg : Project: Whisperish

Sun 13 April 2014

My darling sister is abroad now and I'm in New York talking with robots. All we have between us are our precious emails, since we both mumble too much to hear one another over the telephone. Our emails are full of sisterly secrets, the primordial source of all poetry. So I wrote a program that would take our emails and generate text that is even less sensical. I guess the motivation for this project is wanting to write poetry but in fact being too lazy to write poetry. Here's the code.
