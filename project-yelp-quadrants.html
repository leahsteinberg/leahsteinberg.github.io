Leah Steinberg : Project: Yelp Quadrants

Sun 13 April 2014

I love New York Magazine's approval matrix, and of course there's my lifelong goal of quantifying ~vibes~. So the project I'm working on now will allow you to search Yelp and see the results on a coordinate plane of Introverted - Extroverted, Old News - Undiscovered. We're just cleaning up the visualization now, it should be up soon. It turns out, counting the number of times words like "drunk" and "party" are used in a bunch of yelp reviews can tell you everything about where to never go, and THAT is data. I've been loving learning d3 and hope to keep abusing its data-ish intentions.
